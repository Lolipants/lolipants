Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-26

Lolipants (“we”, “us”) provides a fashion design and ordering experience. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Account data: name, email, auth identifiers.
  • Design content: saved designs, text layers, images you upload for prints.
  • Order data: delivery address, city, phone, notes, order status history.
  • Device & app data: basic diagnostics needed to operate the service.
  • Push notification identifiers: a OneSignal player/device id if you enable notifications.

Camera and photos

The app may request camera access for measurement estimation and to capture images for design uploads. You can deny permission and still use most features.

Third-party AI services

Some Lolipants features use third-party artificial intelligence providers to generate design suggestions, on-model previews, and body measurement estimates. We only send data to these services when you choose an AI feature and agree to data sharing in the app.

Providers we use:

  • Google Gemini (Google LLC) — design image rendering and on-model previews.
  • OpenAI (OpenAI, L.L.C.) — design suggestions from text prompts and AI body measurement analysis from photos you capture.

Data that may be sent when you use an AI feature:

  • Text prompts you enter for design ideas.
  • Design preview images and garment configuration choices.
  • Garment type, colour, and optional gender preference.
  • Camera photos you choose to submit for AI measurement estimation.

Before any data is sent, the app shows a disclosure naming these providers and the types of data involved, and asks for your permission. You can withdraw consent at any time in Settings → Privacy & legal → Third-party AI sharing. After withdrawal, AI features will ask for permission again before sending data.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms and privacy policies. We select providers that offer protections appropriate for the data we send. We do not sell your personal data to AI providers.

How we use data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To save designs and fulfill orders with tailors and couriers.
  • To send transactional updates (e.g., order status) if you opt in.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug reliability issues.

Payments

Payments are processed by a payment provider. We do not store your full card details on our servers.

Sharing

We share data only as needed to provide the service (for example, with tailors/couriers for delivery fulfillment, infrastructure providers for hosting and messaging, and—with your in-app consent—Google Gemini and OpenAI for AI features described above).

Retention

We keep data as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations, then delete or anonymize it where possible.

Your choices

  • Access and update your profile information.
  • Disable push notifications in your device settings.
  • Withdraw third-party AI data-sharing consent in Settings.
  • Request deletion of your account by contacting support.
